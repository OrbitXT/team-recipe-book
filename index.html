Caiden's Chicken and Rice

#

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 lb chicken breast, diced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add diced chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add mixed vegetables to the skillet and cook for another 3-4 minutes until tender.
  4. Stir in cooked rice and soy sauce, mixing well to combine all ingredients.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tom's Steak cubes

Ingredients:

  • Steak cubes
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 50g butter
  • 800g potato
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook steak in a pan with garlic and butter.
  2. Slice potatoes into parts, add the rest of the butter. Cook in oven or airfryer.

Will's tuna mayo sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of tiger, bloomer bread
  • 1 block of unsalted butter
  • 1 tin of tuna chunks in brine
  • Mayonnaise
  • 2 square cuts of cheddar cheese
  • Cucumber

Instructions:

  1. Open the tin of tuna and drain the brine
  2. Empty the tuna chunks into a bowl and apply about 2 tbsp of mayonnaise
  3. Mix the tuna and mayonnaise until there is no pink meat visible
  4. Spread a thin layer of butter onto one side of each slice of bread
  5. Cut 6 thin circles from the cucumber
  6. Place the tuna mix onto one slice of bread
  7. Layer the cucumber onto the tuna mix
  8. Layer the cheese on top of the cucumber

Myles' Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  5. Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  7. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
